Who invented CSS?

Cascading Style Sheet were first proposed by Hakon Wium Lee, a Chief Technical Officer working at CERN with Tim Berners-Lee.
Hakon Lie released the first draft of "Cascading HTML Style Sheet" in 1994 at the same time Brent Bos(developed Argo) was also building a browser that has style sheet that could be used by other markup languages(eg. HTML, VHTML, etc.).
